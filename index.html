Intel i7-6700K and GTX 970 SLI Build

I built this Intel based machine for my brother back in 2015. It has an ASUS Maximus VIII Hero Alpha motherboard, i7-6700K CPU, 16GB GSkill Ripjaws RAM, 256GB Samsung SSD, 2TB Western Digital HDD, Corsair PSU, Corsair H110i AiO, and two MSI GTX 970s in SLI. At the time, it was one of the fastest PCs on the market.

Intel i7-8700K and GTX 1070 FTW2 Builds

After the release of the 8700K last year, I knew it was time for me to upgrade. For this build, I went with the ASUS Maximus X Hero with WiFi. I added 16GB of Corsair RGB RAM, two 256GB Samsung 850 Evo SSDs, two 2TB Western Digital HDDs, Corsair 850W PSU, Corsair H110i AiO and the EVGA GTX 1070 FTW2. For the case, I chose Corsair's 570X.
